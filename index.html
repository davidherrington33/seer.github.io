Designs for the future

What we do

Seer solutions is a project launched in the fall of 2017 by David Herrington. In early 2018, Iyas Fouk Aladeh joined David to work on mechanical design. Seer aims to take common technologies that are used everyday and improve both their efficiency, and their effectiveness. Eventually, Seer aims to become a design company that outsources it's production and sells it's products through sites like Amazon. Seer will be expanded into Software products, but is currently only working on Mechanical Designs.

Fading Hair Clipper by Seer

A major style in the hair industry, fading has become an everyday necessity. Faded hair needs constant maintenance, and it can become costly and time consuming to keep fading it. Currently in the final design stages, the fading hair clipper is a clip on attachment to all hair cutters that easily moves from number 5 to a number 1, enabling you to fade yourself in an effecient and effective manner.
